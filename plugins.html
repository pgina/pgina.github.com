3rd Party Plugins

pGina comes with a lot of plugins out of the box (MySQL, LDAP, Logging, Single User, ...). All of the built-in plugins are documented in our documentation pages. However, the whole point of having a plugin model is so that you, the end user, can choose the method and style of user authentication, authorization and management that you wish to use. This page serves as a listing of those additional choices.
